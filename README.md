# Zero Wait  Food Invnite (ZWIT)


 Project Overview :-

Zero Wait Food Initiative (ZWFI) is a web-based platform designed to reduce food waste by connecting food donors with organizations, volunteers, and individuals who can redistribute surplus food to people in need.

The platform aims to:

Reduce food wastage
Support hunger relief efforts
Enable quick food donation requests
Create a sustainable food-sharing ecosystem
Connect donors and receivers efficiently


 Problem Statement :-

Large quantities of edible food are wasted daily from restaurants, events, hotels, and households, while many people struggle with food insecurity.

ZWFI addresses this issue by providing a digital platform where surplus food can be donated and distributed before it goes to waste.
